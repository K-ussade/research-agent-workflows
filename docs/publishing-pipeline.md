# Publishing Pipeline

## Purpose

This document defines the public-safe pipeline for moving AI-assisted research from draft to publication.

## Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Required Check |
| --- | --- |
| Draft prepared | Research brief is marked `Draft / review-required`. |
| Source reviewed | Candidate sources have review status. |
| Citation checked | Claims tie to citation log entries. |
| Boundary checked | Private and sealed material is excluded. |
| Disclosure prepared | AI assistance disclosure is accurate. |
| Editorial approved | Human reviewer approves the artifact for its use. |
| Published | Public artifact exists and is linked. |

## Publication Rules

- Do not publish automatically.
- Do not publish private research notes.
- Do not publish scraped copyrighted content.
- Do not publish private Foundation operations, donor data, student data, volunteer private data, customer data, private training corpora, security-sensitive details, or sealed YOSO-YAi LLC IP.
- Do not imply client outcomes, publication outcomes, active services, or authoritative AI research claims without review and evidence.

## Publication Record

Each publication record should identify:

- artifact;
- reviewer;
- publication location;
- date;
- status label;
- source list;
- AI assistance disclosure;
- excluded sensitive material.
